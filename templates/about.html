Welcome to About Page Of Your To-Do Web App!

Our Mission

Our mission is to help individuals and teams manage their tasks effectively, prioritize their work, and achieve their goals. We aim to provide a user-friendly interface and essential features that make task management a breeze.

Features:

  • Task Management: Create, edit, and delete tasks with ease.
  • User Authentication: Secure and easy-to-use login and registration system.
  • Responsive Design: Accessible on all devices for seamless task management.
  • Due Dates and Reminders: Stay on top of your schedule with due dates and reminders.
  • Filter and Sort: Easily find and organize your tasks based on different criteria.

Technologies Used:

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Python (Flask)
  • Database: Flask-SQLAlchemy
  • Authentication: JWT (JSON Web Tokens)

Why Choose Our To-Do Web App?

  • User-Friendly: Intuitive design and easy navigation.
  • Secure: Your data is protected with robust security measures.
  • Efficient: Features designed to enhance productivity and time management.
  • Accessible: Use the app on any device, anywhere, anytime.
